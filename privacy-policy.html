Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

HOPPP! ("we", "our", "us") is developed and maintained by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. We built this app on a local-first principle: your behavioral data lives on your device by default.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide: When you create an account, we collect your email address and the goal text you enter. The list of apps you choose to block is stored locally on your device.

Usage data: We collect session data related to app blocking attempts, turn counts, and challenge outcomes. This data is used to power the Goalkeeper feature and may be stored on our servers to deliver the service.

Device information: We may collect device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), operating system version, and app version for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising purposes.

Friend notification data: If you optionally add a friend's phone number for accountability notifications, we store that number solely to deliver SMS messages when triggered. We do not use it for any other purpose.

2. Advertising (AppLovin MAX)

The free tier of HOPPP! displays advertisements served through AppLovin MAX. AppLovin and its advertising partners may collect and use device identifiers and usage data to serve interest-based advertisements.

AppLovin may collect: device identifiers, IP address, general location (country/region), device type and OS, in-app interaction data, and advertising interaction data.

AppLovin's services may involve measurement partners operating under the Open Measurement Working Group standards.

For more information about AppLovin's data practices, visit legal.applovin.com. To opt out of interest-based advertising by AppLovin, visit applovin.com/optout or use your device's advertising opt-out settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the HOPPP! service
  • To power the Goalkeeper challenge feature using your stated goal
  • To send friend accountability SMS notifications when triggered
  • To display advertisements (free tier only)
  • To improve the app through analytics and crash reporting
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • AppLovin MAX for ad delivery (free tier)
  • Third-party service provider to power the Goalkeeper conversation using your goal text and challenge context
  • Firebase / Google for push notifications and crash analytics
  • SMS provider (Twilio) solely for friend accountability messages
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We never share your behavioral data with social media companies, data brokers, or advertisers beyond what is described above.

5. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of interest-based advertising
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To opt out of interest-based ads: Go to your device Settings → Privacy → Ads and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalisation", or reset your Advertising ID.

To delete your account and data: Contact us at privacy@kobar.co. We will delete all server-side data within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

HOPPP! is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 where applicable under local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at privacy@kobar.co and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Friend phone numbers are deleted immediately if you remove the friend link. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

8. GDPR (EEA & UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, our legal bases for processing are: performance of contract (to provide the service), legitimate interests (analytics, fraud prevention), and consent (advertising). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. CCPA (California Residents)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete, the right to opt out of the sale of personal information, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell personal information as defined under CCPA. To submit a request, contact privacy@kobar.co.

10. Australian Privacy Act & New Zealand Privacy Act

If you are located in Australia, we handle your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). You have the right to access and request correction of your personal information. If you believe we have breached the APPs, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

If you are located in New Zealand, we handle your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 2020 and the Information Privacy Principles (IPPs). You have the right to access and request correction of your personal information. If you believe we have interfered with your privacy, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner at privacy.org.nz.

We will not disclose your personal information to overseas recipients without your consent or unless we have taken reasonable steps to ensure the recipient complies with the APPs or IPPs, as applicable.

11. Indonesian Personal Data Protection (UU PDP)

If you are located in Indonesia, we process your personal data in accordance with Law No. 27 of 2022 on Personal Data Protection (UU PDP). As a data controller, we collect and process your personal data based on your explicit consent, which you provide when creating an account and using the service.

Your rights under UU PDP include: the right to be informed about the processing of your data, the right to access your personal data, the right to correct inaccurate data, the right to request deletion of your data, the right to withdraw consent, the right to object to automated decision-making, and the right to lodge a complaint.

Cross-border data transfer: Your personal data may be transferred to and processed on servers located outside Indonesia. We ensure that any such transfer complies with UU PDP requirements, including verifying that the receiving country provides an equivalent level of data protection or that appropriate safeguards are in place.

In the event of a personal data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you and the relevant authorities within 3x24 hours as required by UU PDP. To exercise any of your rights, contact us at privacy@kobar.co.

12. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We will notify you of any breach affecting your personal information as required by applicable law.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notice or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
